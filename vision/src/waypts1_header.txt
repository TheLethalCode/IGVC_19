
How to use waypts1_header ->

	1)define a structure "output"  
	2)call the function "find_waypoint()"  
	3)now the structure contains three parameters-->x,y,angle(in degree)

Eg.->
	
	output a;
	a=find_waypoint();

	***********
	1)Input image of lane(without obstacle) or ransac parameters has to be given
	2)Input image of obstacle(with or without lane) has to be given
	***********

	Description->
	1)Define the stepsize (how much ahead does the bot move).
	2)Define bot_length and bot_width.
	3)Centre point of the bot is given as way point.
